

```python
line = open('Walden.txt').read()      # 打开并读取内容，格式为字符串
words = line.split()     # 去空格，格式为列表
t = {}
for word in words:           # 存入字典，单词为key，统计数字为value
    if word in t:
        t[word]=t[word]+1
    elif word not in t:  
        t[word]=1
sorted(t.items(), key=lambda item:item[1], reverse=True)
```
